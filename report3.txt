IT SECURITY INCIDENT REPORT

Report ID: SEC-0925
Date: August 21, 2025  
Prepared by: InfoSec Team

Incident Summary:
On August 19, 2025, a phishing email was reported by multiple users. The message mimicked internal communication and linked to a malicious credential-harvesting site.

Actions Taken:
- Email quarantined across all accounts.
- Password resets issued to affected users.
- Domain added to blocklist.

Impact:
- 7 user accounts affected
- No data exfiltration detected
- Downtime: None

Next Steps:
- Re-training for staff on phishing detection
- Enhance MFA enforcement for all critical systems

Report Closed: August 22, 2025
